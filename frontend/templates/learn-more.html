How It Works

Vouch Verification Process

1

Submission

Users submit vouches with required evidence including screenshots, transaction details, and contact information of both parties.

2

Verification

Our admin team thoroughly reviews each submission, verifying authenticity through multiple checkpoints.

3

Confirmation

Both parties are contacted to confirm the transaction details and approve the vouch publication.

4

Publication

Once verified, the vouch is published with a verification badge and becomes publicly visible.

File Requirements & Limitations

Screenshots

  • Maximum file size: 5MB per image
  • Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG
  • Minimum resolution: 800x600 pixels
  • Maximum of 5 screenshots per vouch

Text Content

  • Description: 50-500 characters
  • Transaction details: Maximum 1000 characters
  • Contact information required for both parties

Time Limits

  • Submit within 30 days of transaction
  • Verification process: 24-48 hours
  • Edit window: 24 hours after publication
